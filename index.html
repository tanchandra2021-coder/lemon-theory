About Us

More than
lemonade.

Lemon Theory was born from a simple idea: what if your favorite refreshing drink was also doing something good for your body? We're a team of four passionate creators bringing freshly squeezed, probiotic-boosted lemonade to the Suburban Chicago community — one bright cup at a time.

From our moveable truck at Market Square to private events and concerts, we're on a mission to make every sip bright, every guest feel right.
